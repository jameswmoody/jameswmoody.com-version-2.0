Total Eclipse 2017

Witnessing a total solar eclipse has been on my bucket list for many years now. I always assumed I'd have to travel to some remote part of the world to make this goal happen but little did I know I'd be only a few hours away from the best possible location to view the first total eclipse since the 70's. A few friends from college and I plan to travel down to Southern Illinois University's Carbondale campus to make sure we have the best view possible. I'll be sure to post some pictures when I get back.
