My Life as a Developer, Traveler, Human

In the last few years I’ve explored new technologies, offered API solutions to developers from Fortune 500 companies, and worked directly with clients to solve their needs. At the same time I've collected 73 vinyl records, travelled to 38 states, 8 countries, ran 2 half marathons, waited 22 hours on a New York sidewalk with the hope of getting into SNL (I got in!), and even started writing a movie just for the fun of it.

I've never shied away from anything I think might end up being a cool life experience. I've been fortunate enough to do things that I could have only dreamed of during my modest childhood in metro Detroit. I look forward to the next thing life has planned for me and I know that no matter what I experience, there will always be something to learn from it.

Here, I’ll showcase what I’m working on and where I’ve been (both figuratively and literally) as I continue to grow as a developer. Feel free to take a look at my GitHub account as well to get more insight on the contributions I’ve made to the community. If you like what you see, have suggestions, or just feel like talking, shoot me an email.

Recent Projects

TabOut

We all know the struggle of being ready to leave a crowded bar, only to have to fight several other people for the bartender's attention just to close out your tab. Over the last month, I've had the pleasure of leading a team of four developers as we attempt to provide a meaningful solution to this common problem.

So far, we’ve built a mobile-first web app as a proof of concept using Ruby on Rails, Javascript and jQuery, PostgreSQL for our database, and many, many hours of prideful and dedicated work. Additionally, we’ve used Braintree’s API for payment processing and Twilio for notifications.

Rosey's MS Foundation

Rosey's MS Foundation is a not for profit 501 C3 organization, devoted to finding a cure for Multiple Sclerosis and related auto-immune diseases.

As Rosey's Web Developer, I've worked with the Board of Directors to provide various solutions to increase the organizations web presence. This has included building and maintaining the main website, working with Northwestern University's Feinberg School of Medicine, and assisting in social media outreach..

MovieCrate

Looking for all of your Action movies? Want to see something with Samuel L. Jackson? Or maybe 1994 was a good year for you. MovieCrate can be used with a number of keywords and search terms to get to your movies quicker. Built with Ruby, Sinatra, and The Movie Database API.
